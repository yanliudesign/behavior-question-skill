## EN — 面试可直接说的版本（60–90 秒）
**S** Three months into a planned rewrite, my team was committed to replacing our legacy service from scratch.
**T** I started to suspect the rewrite would slip the launch, but the team had already invested heavily and momentum was against changing course.
**A** Instead of arguing from opinion, I spent two days profiling the legacy system and prototyping a targeted fix for the actual bottleneck. I brought the data to our planning meeting, walked through the projected timelines side by side, and acknowledged the sunk cost openly so it didn't feel like blame. I proposed we ship the targeted fix first and defer the rewrite.
**R** The team agreed; we shipped on schedule and avoided roughly six weeks of rework. Looking back, I'd have surfaced the profiling data a week earlier instead of waiting until I was certain.

## 中文要点 — 复盘自查用
- **背景**：重写遗留服务的方案已投入 3 个月，团队势头很强。
- **我的任务/难点**：我判断重写会拖期，但要逆着沉没成本和团队情绪去说服。
- **关键动作与判断**：不靠嘴仗——花 2 天 profiling + 做针对性原型，用数据并排对比时间线，主动点破沉没成本避免变成指责。
- **结果（数字）**：按期上线，避免约 6 周返工。
- **如果重来**：profiling 数据该早一周拿出来，不必等到自己 100% 确定。
